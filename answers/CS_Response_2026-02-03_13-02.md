### Verified Facts
* A database typically has a schema, which defines the structure of the data.
* Databases can be categorized into different types, such as relational, NoSQL, graph, and time-series databases.
* Database management systems (DBMS) are software that interact with the database to perform various operations, such as create, read, update, and delete (CRUD).
