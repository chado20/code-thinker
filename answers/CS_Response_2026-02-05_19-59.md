## Direct Answer
Google is a multinational technology company specializing in Internet-related services and products.

## Explanation
Google's primary function revolves around its search engine, which uses algorithms to retrieve and rank web pages in response to user queries. Beyond search, Google develops and invests in a wide range of technologies, including cloud computing, advertising, software, and hardware.

## Verified Facts
* Google was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin.
* The company is headquartered in Mountain View, California.
* Google's initial public offering (IPO) in 2004 raised $1.67 billion.
